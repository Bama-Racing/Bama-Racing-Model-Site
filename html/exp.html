Noteworthy Life Experiences

French Exchange Program

Growing up, I was very fortunate to have the opportunity to participate in an international exchange with a French family. Not only am I now able to speak reliable conversational French, but I truly believe this exchange broadened my world view and made me a more independing and adventurous individual.

Scouting

Possibly one of the largest impacts on my character has been my Scout Troop & Venture Crew; the Scout Motto & Law are two things I hold very near to my heart, along with all the memories. I completed the rank of Eagle Scout in October of 2017.

Family

Since about the age of three, up until the first day of my high school engineering course, I wanted to be a pilot. Throughout those 15 plus years, and now in the outset of my engineering career, I have always been supported by my parents and two sisters, extended family, and friends. I can never thank them enough.
